# Hand Gesture Instructions for Digit Recognition

This document shows how to perform hand gestures to represent digits 0-9.

## Digit Gestures

### Digit 0
- Make a fist with all fingers closed
- No fingers extended

### Digit 1
- Extend only your index finger
- Keep all other fingers closed

### Digit 2
- Extend your index and middle fingers (like a "V" or peace sign)
- Keep thumb, ring, and pinky fingers closed

### Digit 3
- Extend your thumb, index, and middle fingers
- Keep ring and pinky fingers closed

### Digit 4
- Extend your index, middle, ring, and pinky fingers
- Keep thumb closed

### Digit 5
- Extend all five fingers (open hand)
- Show your palm to the camera

### Digit 6
- Extend your thumb, index, and pinky fingers
- Keep middle and ring fingers closed
- Similar to "rock on" or "I love you" sign

### Digit 7
- Extend your thumb, index, middle, and ring fingers
- Keep pinky finger closed

### Digit 8
- Extend your thumb, middle, ring, and pinky fingers
- Keep index finger closed

### Digit 9
- Extend your thumb and pinky fingers
- Keep index, middle, and ring fingers closed
- Similar to "hang loose" or "shaka" sign

## Tips for Accurate Recognition

1. Position your hand clearly in front of the camera
2. Maintain good lighting conditions
3. Hold your gestures steadily
4. Try to keep your hand upright with fingers pointing up
5. Make sure extended fingers are clearly extended and folded fingers are clearly folded
6. Keep your hand at a moderate distance from the camera - not too close or too far
